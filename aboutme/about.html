Born and raised in Miami Florida, Amanda Alvarez is a senior majoring in broadcast journalism at the University of Miami.

She has always been interested in television, it all began at Ethel Koger Beckham Elementary school where she started working on the television program called the "Best News". Every Morning she reported on the lunch menu and afterschool activities that were scheduled to take place.

Her passion for journalism expanded when she started middle school where she frequently wrote for the school's newspaper and worked on the yearbook during her time there.

Meanwhile, Amanda has been involved in cheerleading and dancing since the age of 5, she has competed at many competitions locally and nationally receiving accolades at almost all of them. Her talent extends beyond the dance floor. She also cheered competitively through her elementary and middle school career, she cheered for her high school's football and basketball teams. She is currently a cheerleader at the University of Miami as well as a member of the KAOS hip-hop team.
