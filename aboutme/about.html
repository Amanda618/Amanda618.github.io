Born and raised in Miami Florida, Amanda Alvarez is a junior majoring in broadcast journalism at the University of Miami.

She has always been interetsed in television, it all began at Ethel Koger Beckham Elementary school where she started working on the televison program called the "Best News". Every Morning she reported on the lunch menu and afterschool asctivities that were scheduled to take place.

Her passion for journalism expanded when she started middle school where she frequently wrote for the schools newspaper and worked on the yearbook during her time there.

Meanwhile, Amanda has been involved in cheerleading and dancing since the age of 5, she has competed at many competitions locally and nationally receiveing acollades at almost all of them. Her talent extends beyond the dance floor. She also cheered competively through her elementary and middle school career, she cheered for her high schools football and basketball teams. She is currently a cheerleader at the university of Miami as well as a memeber of the KAOS hip-hop team.
